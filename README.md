Rocket Drone FPV Frame – 3D Printable

A unique rocket‑style FPV drone frame designed for speed, style, and easy 3D printing. The aerodynamic body protects your electronics while giving the drone an aggressive rocket look in the air.

The frame is lightweight, modular, and easy to assemble, making it perfect for FPV builders who want something different from standard quad frames.

Features

Rocket‑inspired aerodynamic design
Fully 3D printable frame
Lightweight and strong structure
Modular parts for easy replacement
Space for FPV camera and electronics
Printing

Recommended: PLA+, PETG, or ABS
0.2 mm layer height
20–40% infill
Included
